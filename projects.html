Iron Horse Antiques

What it is:

This is a web application for my Aunt and Uncle's antique business. Users can sign up for their mailing list, find information about the business, see current items for sale, and check out the family's partner businesses.

Why:

My Aunt and Uncle run an antique shop in Northern Illinois and the website was comically outdated. I decided it needed a new look and built them a better one!

Self-Guided Architectural Tour of Chicago

What it is:

Users can choose notable Chicago architectural sites from a list to add to their self-guided tour route. The Google Maps Directions service will optimize all of the waypoints and return a map of the shortest route between all sites.

Why:

Chicago has some of the most architecturally unique buildings in the world, but it would be impossible to admire all the buildings in one day. So I created this app allowing users to pick and choose which architectural sites they wanted to see. This app creates an optimized route to take you to your selected sites with the shortest possible route.

Vacation Affordability Judge

What it is:

Vacation Affordability Judge allows users to search for their prospective destination city, and receive an "Affordability Grade" for that city.

Why:

I love vacations, but they can be pricey. I built this app to give users a general idea of how expensive their prospective travel destination can be.
