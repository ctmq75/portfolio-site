Projects

Iron Horse Antiques

This is a web application for my Aunt and Uncle's antique business. Users are able to sign up for their mailing list, find information about the business, see current items for sale, and check out the family's partner businesses.

Self-Guided Architechtural Tour of Chicago

Users are able to choose notable Chicago architectural sites from a list to add to their self-guided tour route. The Google Maps Directions service will optimize all of the waypoints and return a map of the shortest route between all sites.

Vacation Affordibility Judge

Vacation Affordibility Jugde allows users to search for their prospective destination city, and receive an "Affordibility Grade" for that city.

Bears Quiz App

Users are asked a series of multiple choice questions about the history of the Chicago Bears. Users are given a final score once all questions are answered.
